📋 Module 4.1

SA Showing Script

⏱ 10 min 🏅 Milestone: Showing Ready

💰 Why this matters: A polished showing = investor confidence = higher bids = bigger commissions. Your showing script is the engine that drives deal execution.

📖 Learn

The Complete SA Showing Script

Follow this exact sequence every time. Consistency builds investor trust and keeps the process tight.

  1. "Thanks for coming out!"
    Greet warmly. Set a professional but friendly tone.
  2. "Before we walk the property, do you have any questions?"
    Handle questions up front so the walkthrough is uninterrupted.
  3. "If you have questions while walking, wait till we conclude our tour."
    This keeps the showing on YOUR timeline.
  4. Walk the property together.
    Stay WITH your investor. Don't let them wander alone.
  5. "What do you think?"
    Open-ended — let them talk. Listen more than you speak.
  6. "Did you have any offer price in mind?"
    Get their number first. Never anchor.
  7. "Are you aware of the double closing costs?"
    Set expectations on cost structure early.
  8. "The deposit will need to be delivered within one hour — cashier's check or wire."
    Urgency + specificity = serious bidders only.
  9. "Submit the bid where if it was $1 more, you'd walk away."
    This gets their REAL highest number.
  10. "The AA will announce the winner within 15 minutes."
    Creates urgency and a clean process.
  11. "Are you able to send a wire within an hour?"
    Final qualification — are they truly ready to close?

⚡ Pro Tips

  • Walk WITH your investor — never let them go ahead without you.
  • Don't give too much info about the house inside — keep that for outside.
  • Chat about numbers and logistics outside, never in front of the seller.
  • Stay calm, confident, and conversational — not salesy.
🔨 Do

Practice the Showing Script

  1. Read through the full script out loud — 3 complete run-throughs.
  2. Practice transitioning between each step naturally (no pauses or fumbling).
  3. Pair up with a partner and role-play: one person is the SA, the other is the investor.
  4. Switch roles and identify awkward spots in delivery.
✅ Prove

🎤 Record a Mock Showing

Record yourself running the full SA Showing Script from start to finish — no notes allowed. You should hit every step of the script naturally.

Submission: Upload your recording or deliver live to your Team Lead for sign-off.
