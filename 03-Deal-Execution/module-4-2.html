MIOSI$ Framework

⏱ 8 min 🏅 Milestone: Closer Mindset

💰 Why this matters: MIOSI$ is the difference between an average agent and a closer — it works on EVERY appointment. Master this and you'll close more deals than anyone on the team.

📖 Learn

The MIOSI$ Framework

Six letters. One system. Works on every seller appointment. Internalize each component and apply them in order.

M

Motivation

WHY are they selling? You must uncover the real reason. It's rarely what they say first.

Financial WoesForeclosureInheritanceRelocation DeathDivorceDisabilityDisagreement

The 4 D's — Death, Divorce, Disability, Disagreement — are the most common deep motivators.

IO

Isolate the Objection

Pinpoint the ONE thing holding them back. If you can solve it, the deal moves.

"If it wasn't for that, would you move forward?"

"What's holding you back?"

SI

Statement of Impact

Connect your solution back to their pain. Make them FEEL the relief.

"We are here with you every step of the way."

"This is going to take care of [their pain point]."

$

Money

Paint the financial picture clearly. Show them what they'll walk away with.

"This should take care of the liens and back taxes."

"You'll walk away with $X."

🔨 Do

Write MIOSI$ Responses for 3 Scenarios

For each scenario, write out what you'd say for M, IO, SI, and $.

Scenario 1: Inherited Property

Seller inherited a home from their mother 2 years ago. It's in rough shape, they live out of state, and property taxes are piling up. They've had two other "investors" fall through.

Scenario 2: Divorce

A couple is divorcing. Both want to sell fast but can't agree on a price. There's a lien from an old HVAC contractor. One spouse is cooperative, the other is hostile.

Scenario 3: Pre-Foreclosure

Homeowner lost their job 6 months ago. They're 3 months behind on mortgage. The house needs about $40K in repairs. They want to save their credit if possible.
