📋 Module 4.7

Addenda & Pre-Close Procedures

⏱ 10 min 🏅 Milestone: Process Master

💰 Why this matters: A smooth pre-close process = happy investors who come back for more. Messy paperwork and missed steps cost you repeat business and referrals.

📖 Learn

Addenda Types

Addenda modify or supplement the TREC contract. Know when to use each one.

  • A

    Addendum for Property Subject to Mandatory Membership

    Required when the property is in an HOA. Includes HOA transfer fees, outstanding dues, and resale certificate info.

  • B

    Seller's Temporary Residential Lease (Leaseback)

    When the seller needs to stay after closing. Include specific move-out date and daily rate. Push for leaseback deposit — especially for hoarder houses.

  • C

    Amendment

    Used to change terms after contract execution. Option period extensions must include a $ figure. Both parties must sign.

  • D

    Third-Party Financing Addendum

    Used when the buyer is financing. Specifies loan type, terms, and deadlines. Less common for our cash deals but know it.

🔑 Key Rule: Amendments

Any amendment for an option period extension must have a dollar figure attached. "Free" extensions don't exist — the seller needs compensation for the delay.

📖 Learn

Pre-Close Procedure Checklist

After the winning bid — here's what happens, in order.

  1. Send executed contract to title company — Immediately after all signatures are collected.
  2. Deliver earnest money — Wire or cashier's check to the title company per contract terms.
  3. Complete SIS sheet — Seller Information Sheet with all deal details for internal tracking.
  4. Collect lender/LLC documentation — Buyer's entity info, operating agreement, authorization to close.
  5. Coordinate closing timeline — Confirm expected close date with title company, buyer, and seller.
  6. Send DocuSign documents — All remaining docs that need e-signatures.
  7. Confirm title clear — Title company confirms no liens, encumbrances, or issues blocking close.
  8. Fund and close — Funding can take 24-48 hours. Set this expectation with the seller.

⏰ Under Promise, Over Deliver

Set the expectation with the seller that funding can take 24-48 hours. If it happens faster, you're a hero. If it takes the full 48, you set the right expectation.
