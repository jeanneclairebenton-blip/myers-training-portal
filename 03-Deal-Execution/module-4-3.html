P.O.E.T. Framework

⏱ 8 min 🏅 Milestone: Competition Eliminator

💰 Why this matters: P.O.E.T. disqualifies your competition WITHOUT badmouthing anyone. It positions Myers as the real deal while letting pretenders eliminate themselves.

📖 Learn

The P.O.E.T. Standard

The Reality

"95% of people who call themselves investors aren't actual investors." Radio ads teach people to tie up properties without ever closing. A contract without P.O.E.T. doesn't give peace of mind.

There are two types of investors — those that contract a property and those that can actually close. P.O.E.T. is how sellers tell the difference.

P

Proof of Funds

From a reputable bank. Not a screenshot — an actual bank letter.

Verified bank letter
O

Option Period

10 days or less, with a fee attached. Long option periods = red flag.

≤ 10 days + fee
E

Earnest Money

At least $5,000. Serious money proves serious intent.

Minimum $5,000
T

TREC Contract

No custom contracts. TREC is the Texas standard — it protects everyone.

Standard TREC only

How to Present P.O.E.T. to Sellers

You say:

"There are two types of investors — those that contract a property and those that can actually close. You've probably heard the radio ads: 'We buy houses!' But here's the truth — most of them tie up properties and never close.

"We do things differently. We bring Proof of funds from a reputable bank. Our Option period is 10 days or less. We put down at least $5,000 in Earnest money. And we only use the standard TREC contract — no funny business. That's how you know we're real."

🔨 Do

Practice the P.O.E.T. Delivery

  1. Read the talk track above 3 times out loud.
  2. Pair up with a partner — one is the agent, the other plays a skeptical seller.
  3. The "seller" should ask: "How do I know you're not like the last guy who backed out?"
  4. Deliver the P.O.E.T. explanation naturally without reading.

🎤 Record Your P.O.E.T. Explanation

Record yourself explaining P.O.E.T. as if you're sitting across from a real seller. 60 seconds max. You should hit all four letters clearly and naturally.
