📋 Module 4.4

The Disarming Statement

⏱ 5 min 🏅 Milestone: Tone Setter

💰 Why this matters: This one statement sets the tone for the entire appointment — get it right and everything else flows. It removes pressure from the seller and makes them trust you immediately.

📖 Learn

The Disarming Statement — Word for Word

Deliver this EXACTLY as written. Every word is intentional. Practice until it sounds natural, not scripted.

"We're going to tour the property together [Name], and, after that, if it sounds like we can work together, that's great. We're excited for the potential of helping you with an easy sale. That's certainly the goal. pause But, if it turns out we can't work together, that's okay too. I promise it won't hurt my feelings. At the end of the day, we just want a win/win situation." Then tie down: "Will that work for you?" or "Fair enough?"

Why this works: The disarming statement removes all sales pressure. It tells the seller "I'm not here to push you" — which paradoxically makes them MORE likely to work with you. The pause before "But" is critical — it lets the first part sink in.

🔨 Do

Practice Word-for-Word — 5 Times

  1. Read the statement out loud slowly — focus on getting every word right.
  2. Deliver it again, this time adding the pause before "But."
  3. Third time: add the body language (stand at angle, hands in front, eyes up).
  4. Fourth time: deliver to a mirror. Watch your own body language.
  5. Fifth time: deliver to a partner. Get feedback on tone, pacing, and authenticity.
✅ Prove

🎤 Record the Disarming Statement

Record yourself delivering the Disarming Statement. It must be near-verbatim — every key phrase should be there. Include the tie-down at the end.

Grading criteria: Word accuracy, natural delivery, proper pause, correct body language positioning, tie-down included.
