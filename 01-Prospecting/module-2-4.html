Skip Tracing / Likely Buyers Script

"Hey [Name], I found your info through some property research I was doing in [area]. It looks like you've been active with investment properties. We source off-market deals for investors — are you currently buying?"

Discovery Questions

Once they're engaged, learn about them with these questions:

🎯 Strategy
What's your investment strategy — flips, rentals, or both?
📍 Area
What areas are you focusing on right now?
💰 Funding
How do you typically fund your deals — cash, hard money, conventional?
⏰ Timeline
When are you looking to close on your next deal?
📊 Volume
How many flips have you done this year?
🏠 Portfolio
How many rental doors do you currently have?

"Our Process" — How to Explain Myers

When investors ask how Myers works, use these talking points:

  • Showings: "We schedule one-time group showings. You walk through, assess the property, and decide."
  • Bidding: "After the showing, you submit a silent bid. Your number stays completely private."
  • Deposits: "Once we accept an offer, there's a standard earnest money deposit to secure the contract."
  • Contracts: "We use a standard Texas contract. Clear title guaranteed through our title company."
💡 Sales Questions to Qualify: "How many flips have you done?" and "How many doors do you have?" tell you instantly whether you're talking to a beginner or a seasoned buyer. Adjust your pitch accordingly.
