Cold Prospecting Script — Team Honey Badger Style

Cold calls are different from warm calls. These people don't know you, haven't seen your emails, and aren't expecting your call. Your job: earn their attention in 10 seconds.

"Hey [Name], my name is [Your Name].

I work with a local investment company that finds 3–5 deals a week for investors in the [City/Area] market.

We source off-market properties — stuff that's not on the MLS — and bring them directly to cash buyers and investors.

Are you actively looking at deals right now, or is this something you'd be open to hearing about?"

Driving for Dollars Script

When you find a property in the field that looks like an investor opportunity:

"Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] with Myers Home Buyers.

We noticed your property at [address] and were curious if you'd consider an offer on it.

We work with a network of cash buyers, and we can typically close in 2–3 weeks. We pay top dollar, cover closing costs, and keep the process completely transparent.

Would you be open to discussing it?"

💡 Key Difference: On cold calls, you'll get more "no's" and hang-ups. That's normal. The goal isn't to close on the first call — it's to open a door. Even a "not right now" is a future follow-up. Log everything in Zoho.

Do

Practice Both Scripts

  1. Read the cold/scraped list script 3 times out loud
  2. Read the Driving for Dollars script 3 times out loud
  3. Deliver each one without looking — focus on sounding natural and direct
Challenge: Time yourself. A good cold script delivery is 20–30 seconds. Any longer and you lose them.
