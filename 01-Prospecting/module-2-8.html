Objections & Rebuttals

📘 Module 2.8 ⏱ 10 min 🏅 Objection Handler
💰 Why This Matters

Every objection you can handle = one more deal that doesn't walk away. The difference between a good agent and a great agent is what happens when the investor says "no" or pushes back.

📖
Learn

The Top 8 Objections (and Exactly How to Handle Them)

You will hear these objections. It's not a matter of "if" — it's "when." Memorize the rebuttals so they become automatic.

1
"The market is slowing down…"
✅ Your Rebuttal
"I hear you — what have you been seeing in your area? Here's what we're seeing: there are opportunities in every market. When the market slows, motivated sellers come out. Our investors are finding some of the best margins right now because there's less competition. Would you rather buy when everyone else is buying, or when the deals are actually good?"
2
"I don't like working with wholesalers."
✅ Your Rebuttal
"I totally understand — and honestly, most wholesalers don't operate the way we do. Would you be mad if I found you a deal at your price? We have over 200 five-star Google reviews. We're licensed agents running a professional operation. If the numbers work for you, does it matter where the deal came from?"
3
"I can't make it to the showing."
✅ Your Rebuttal
"I get it — you're busy. But let me ask you this: is $XX,000 in profit worth 2 hours of your time? Our showings are one-time events. You walk through, assess the property, and submit your bid. If it doesn't work, you've lost 2 hours. If it does, you've found your next deal."
4
"I don't do showings / I buy sight unseen."
✅ Your Rebuttal
"Understood. Here's what's great about our process — silent bids. You walk the property, submit your number, and your bid stays completely private. No bidding wars, no pressure. Your number is your number. And you can send a rep if you can't attend personally."
5
"Your ARV / comps are off."
✅ Your Rebuttal
"That's a fair point — what do you have it at? I'd love to see your numbers. Here's why ours might differ: Zillow uses algorithms. We use actual MLS data — recent sold comps, active listings, and pending contracts. If your numbers are better, I want to learn from that. Let's compare."
6
"I only buy at 70% of ARV."
✅ Your Rebuttal
"Totally respect that. Where are you finding those deals consistently? Honestly, if you can find 70% ARV deals all day, I'll buy those too. In today's market, most of our investors are buying at 75–80% and still making strong returns. The key is volume and speed — we bring you 3–5 deals per week."
7
"I don't want to pay double close costs."
✅ Your Rebuttal
"Great question. Our closing costs typically run 2–2.5%, which is standard. And here's the upside: a double close guarantees you get clear title. There's no daisy-chain, no assignment risk. You're buying directly from us with a clean chain of title. That's worth the small premium."
8
"I have my own title company."
✅ Your Rebuttal
"I appreciate that. Our terms are set with our title company to ensure a smooth, standardized process for everyone. Escrow fees are only $250, and we handle all the coordination. It keeps things clean and fast for both sides."
The Pattern: Notice every rebuttal follows the same structure — Acknowledge → Reframe → Redirect to Value. Never argue. Never get defensive. Always bring it back to the deal.
🔨
Do

Role-Play: 3 Random Objections

Grab a partner (or use a timer and respond to yourself). Have someone throw 3 random objections at you from the list above. Respond in real-time without looking at the rebuttals.

💡 Practice Method: Write each objection on a separate card or sticky note. Shuffle them. Pull one, start your timer, and respond within 10 seconds. The goal is speed and confidence, not perfection.
