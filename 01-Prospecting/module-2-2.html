Certificate 2 — Prospecting Specialist

Warm Prospect Script

📘 Module 2.2 ⏱ 10 min 🏅 Warm Caller
💰 Why This Matters

Warm calls convert 3–5× better than cold calls. Mastering this script means more sit-downs, more deals, and more commissions — because these prospects already know who you are.

📖
Learn

The Warm Script Flow

Every warm call follows a 6-step structure. Master the flow and the words will come naturally:

1
Opening
Warm greeting — use their name, create connection
2
Who You Are
Introduce yourself and the company
3
What You Do
Explain the Myers model in one sentence
4
Value Proposition
Why should they care? Lead with their benefit.
5
Interest Check
Ask if this sounds like something they'd be interested in
6
Appointment Set
Lock in the meeting — offer 2 specific times
"Hey [Name], this is [Your Name].

I work with an investment company called Myers Home Buyers. We source off-market properties for investors — essentially, we find deals that aren't on the MLS and bring them directly to buyers like you.

The reason I'm reaching out is we typically find 3–5 solid deals per week, and we're always looking for serious investors to partner with.

Is that something you'd be open to hearing more about?

Great — I'd love to set up a quick 15-minute call to walk you through how we work. Would [Day] at [Time] or [Day] at [Time] work better for you?"
💡 Pro Tips:
• Stand up while calling — it gives your voice more energy
• Smile — they can literally hear it in your tone
• Have Zoho open — be ready to log the call and notes immediately
• Mirror their pace — match their speed and energy level
🔨
Do

Practice the Script

  1. Read the script 3 times — focus on flow, not memorization
  2. Deliver it out loud 2 times without looking at the script
  3. Don't worry about perfection — focus on sounding natural and confident
Practice Tip: Record yourself on your phone. Listen back. Does it sound like you're reading or having a conversation? Aim for conversational.
✅
Prove

🎤 Record Yourself

Record yourself delivering the complete warm prospect script from memory — no notes, no peeking.

  • Use your phone's voice recorder or a screen recording
  • Aim for a natural, conversational tone
  • It doesn't need to be word-for-word — the flow matters most
🔁
Review — Spaced Repetition
Day 3
Deliver the warm script from memory again
Day 7
Role-play with a partner — have them respond as an investor
Day 14
Use the script on a real warm call
