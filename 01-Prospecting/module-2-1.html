Key Principle: Your pipeline is your lifeline. Agents who prospect daily — even just 30 minutes — close consistently. Agents who only prospect when they "feel like it" go through feast-or-famine cycles.

How They Work Together

Think of these channels as a stacking system:

  • Digital channels (email clicks, skip tracing, Corp Wiki) generate raw leads at scale
  • Data scrapes (hard money lender, property tax) find active investors with real buying history
  • Relationship channels (referrals, networking, Facebook groups) produce the highest-converting leads
  • Field work (Driving for Dollars) uncovers off-market inventory before anyone else

The Daily Prospecting Habit

Top-performing agents dedicate the first 1–2 hours of every day to prospecting. This means:

  1. Review Zoho for new email click leads (verified first)
  2. Make 15–25 calls from your prospecting list
  3. Send 10+ personalized texts from templates
  4. Check one data scrape source for new contacts
