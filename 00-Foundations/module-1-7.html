💰 Why This Matters

Learn

Core Knowledge

Slack = Your Money Channel

Slack is where deals happen in real time. When a new investor lead drops, it shows up in Slack. The agent who responds first wins the opportunity. This isn't optional — this is where deals are made or lost.

🚨

Notifications are MANDATORY

You must have Slack on both your laptop AND your phone with push notifications turned ON. Missing a Slack notification is the same as missing a deal.

Your Two Essential Channels

💰

#myers

The main team communication channel. This is where investor leads drop, deal updates are shared, showing times are posted, and team announcements go out. The fastest responder to a lead wins it.

⚡ Critical — Monitor Always
❓

#askmeanything2026

Your safe space to ask questions, get help, and learn from the team. No question is too basic. This is where new agents get support from experienced agents and leadership.

🤝 Learning & Support

Key Slack Setup Tasks

Complete all of these to be fully connected:

  • 📱 Download Slack on both laptop AND phone
  • 📞 Add phone number to your Slack profile
  • 🔔 Enable push notifications — Settings → Notifications → All new messages
  • 📌 Find the Resource Hub in quick links — this has offer submission links and key docs
  • 🖼 Add a professional profile photo

Where to Find the Resource Hub

In Slack, check the channel bookmarks / pinned items in #myers. The Resource Hub contains:

  • 📋 Offer submission links
  • 📄 Key documents and templates
  • 🔗 Quick access to Zoho, Google Drive, and other tools
