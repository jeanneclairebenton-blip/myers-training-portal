Deal & Financial Terms

ARV After Repair Value
The estimated market value of a property AFTER all repairs and renovations are completed. This is what the investor expects the home to be worth when it's fixed up.
CMA Comparative Market Analysis
A report comparing similar recently sold properties to determine a home's fair market value. Used to price properties and validate ARV.
GP Gross Profit
The difference between what Myers buys the property for and what the investor pays. Your commission is calculated from this number.
Spread
The difference between the purchase price and the ARV — how much potential profit exists in a deal for the investor.
Double Close
Two back-to-back closings on the same day. Myers buys from the seller (Transaction A) then sells to the investor (Transaction B) through the title company.
Leaseback
An arrangement where the seller sells their home but continues to live in it for a set period, paying rent to the new owner.
EMD Earnest Money Deposit
A deposit (typically $5K at Myers) paid by the buyer to show serious intent. At Myers, this is collected within 1 hour of bid acceptance.
NOI Net Operating Income
Total rental income minus operating expenses (not including mortgage). Used by investors evaluating rental properties.
Cap Rate
Capitalization Rate — NOI divided by property value. Used to compare the return potential of investment properties.
Absorption Rate
How quickly homes are selling in a given market area — tells you if it's a buyer's or seller's market.

Myers Acronyms & Frameworks

D4D Driving for Dollars
Physically driving through neighborhoods looking for distressed properties — boarded windows, overgrown yards, vacant signs.
MIOSI$ Motivation–Isolate–Statement of Impact–Money
The Myers conversation framework for seller appointments. Uncover motivation, isolate problems, state impact, then talk numbers.
P.O.E.T. Proof of funds–Option period–Earnest money–TREC
The 4 things you need from every investor before accepting their bid.
F.O.R.D. Family–Occupation–Recreation–Dreams
Rapport-building framework. Ask about these 4 topics to connect with sellers on a personal level.
Big 5
The 5 major systems to inspect: Roof, Foundation, HVAC, Electrical, Plumbing. These are the expensive repairs investors care most about.

Documents & Systems

TREC 1-4 Texas Real Estate Commission Contract
The standard Texas residential contract used for 1-4 family properties. This is what gets sent through DocuSign after bid acceptance.
MVP Agreement
Myers' proprietary listing/services agreement signed by sellers at the appointment.
SIS Sheet
Seller Information Sheet — the intake form capturing all property and seller details at the appointment.
Skip Trace
Finding a property owner's contact information (phone, email) when it's not publicly available. Used for cold outreach on distressed properties.
