Setup Guide

Step-by-Step: Get Set Up

Complete each step below to get your Myers Google Workspace fully configured. This should take about 5 minutes.

Activate Your Myers Gmail

Log into your Myers Gmail account at mail.google.com using the credentials provided by your Team Lead. Change your password immediately to something secure.

Set Up Your Professional Email Signature

Go to Settings → See all settings → General → Signature. Create a new signature with your full name, title, phone number, and the Myers Home Buyers logo.

Join Shared Google Drives

Accept the invitations (or request access from your Team Lead) to join these shared drives:

📁 Myers Deals — Active deal files and documents
📁 Agent Resources — Templates, scripts, training materials
📁 Marketing Drive — Approved marketing assets and property photos

Subscribe to Company Calendars

Add these shared calendars to your Google Calendar:

📅 Company Calendar — Team meetings, training sessions, events
📅 DFW Showings Calendar — All upcoming property showings (critical!)
