Core Knowledge

The Myers Home Buyers Story

Myers Home Buyers was founded in the DFW Metroplex as a wholesale real estate company with a clear mission: help homeowners sell distressed properties fast while giving real estate agents a real path to building wealth.

Our tagline — "Where Agents Become Investors" — isn't just marketing. It's our operating system. At Myers, you don't just earn commissions. You learn the wholesale business from the inside out, building skills and capital to become an investor yourself.

What We Actually Do

  • Buy distressed properties — homes that are tired, behind on payments, or need significant repair
  • Create transparent showing processes — every investor sees the same property at the same time
  • Connect sellers with qualified investors — matching motivated sellers to our buyer network
  • Double close transactions — we buy and sell same-day through title companies

Our Track Record

Hundreds of deals closed. 200+ positive Google reviews. That reputation wasn't built by accident — it was built by agents like you showing up professionally, over-disclosing, and treating every seller like a person, not a transaction.

🔑 Key Insight

We don't sell houses. We sell PEACE OF MIND.

Most wholesalers are transaction machines — they lock up contracts and flip them without caring about the seller's experience. Myers is different. We over-disclose, we bring professionalism to every interaction, and we've built a brand that sellers actually trust. That's why we have 200+ Google reviews. That's why agents come to us — because the deals are real and the process is clean.

As a Myers agent, you earn commissions on every deal. But more importantly, you're learning the wholesale real estate business from the inside. You'll learn how to find deals, negotiate with sellers, work with investors, and understand the numbers. Eventually, you'll have the skills and capital to start investing yourself. This isn't just a job — it's a launchpad.
