💰 Why This Matters

Every deal runs through Zoho. If you can't navigate it confidently, you'll miss leads, lose track of prospects, and watch commissions walk out the door.

Learn

Core Knowledge

Zoho Is Your Pipeline

Zoho CRM is the central nervous system of every Myers deal. Every lead, every call, every prospect, every property — it all lives in Zoho. Master this tool and you'll always know exactly where your deals stand.

The 5 Key Modules

📥

Leads

Incoming seller inquiries. Raw contacts that haven't been qualified yet.

🎯

Prospects

Qualified sellers who have been contacted and show potential.

🏠

Pending Properties

Properties under contract moving toward closing.

📊

Dashboards

Your performance metrics, KPIs, and pipeline at a glance.

📢

Campaigns

Marketing campaigns tracking lead sources and ROI.

Key Actions You'll Do Every Day

  • ➕
    Adding Leads

    Every new seller contact gets entered as a lead with name, phone, property address, and source.

  • 🔄
    Converting to Prospects

    When a lead is qualified (motivated seller, viable property), convert them to a prospect.

  • 📝
    Logging Call Notes

    Every call gets a note. What was discussed, next steps, follow-up date. No exceptions.

  • 🏠
    Creating Pending Properties

    When a listing agreement is signed, create the pending property record.

  • 🔍
    Setting Up Saved Searches

    Create filtered views to build call lists — e.g., "All leads not contacted in 7 days."

  • 📧
    Using Email Filters

    Filter your email-sourced leads to create targeted call lists for daily prospecting.

⚠️ Critical Rule

Log into Zoho on BOTH desktop AND mobile. You need access everywhere, all the time.

🔨

Do

Hands-On Setup

⚡ Log into Zoho CRM on both desktop and mobile, then navigate to each of the 5 modules. Check off each task as you complete it:

Log into Zoho on Desktop

Go to crm.zoho.com and sign in with your Myers credentials. Bookmark this page.

Download & Log into Zoho Mobile App

Download "Zoho CRM" from the App Store or Google Play. Sign in with the same credentials.

Navigate to All 5 Modules

Click through: Leads → Prospects → Pending Properties → Dashboards → Campaigns. Get familiar with each view.

Create a Saved Search

In the Leads module, create a saved search/filter. Example: "All leads added in the last 7 days."

✅

Prove

Team Lead Verification

Practical test: Add a test lead and convert it to a prospect. Your Team Lead will verify this was done correctly.
