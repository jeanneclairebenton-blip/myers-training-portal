Protect the Brand, Protect Your Pipeline

💰 Why This Matters

Protecting the brand protects your deal pipeline. One bad review can cost thousands in lost deals. One professional impression can create a raving fan who refers you three more sellers.

Core Knowledge

The Myers Standard: Transparency Above All

At Myers, we don't hide things. We OVER DISCLOSE. When you're transparent with sellers, investors, and everyone in between, you build trust. Trust builds repeat business. Repeat business builds wealth.

🔑 Core Principle

We create RAVING FANS — not just satisfied customers. 200+ Google reviews prove it works.

Non-Negotiable Conduct Rules

  • 👔
    Professional appearance at ALL times. You represent Myers the moment you leave your house. Dress like someone who handles six-figure transactions.
  • 🚫
    No sunglasses during appointments. Eye contact builds trust. Sunglasses create a barrier between you and the seller.
  • 🚫
    No Bluetooth earpieces during appointments. Give the seller your full, undivided attention. Nothing says "I'm not important to you" like a blinking earpiece.
  • 💼
    Business cards at every meeting. Always carry Myers business cards. Hand them out proactively — to sellers, neighbors, anyone you meet.
  • 📋
    Portfolio at every appointment. Your Myers portfolio demonstrates professionalism and gives the seller confidence in our process.
  • ⭐
    Ask for reviews. After every positive interaction, ask for a Google review. This is how we maintain 200+ reviews and counting.
  • 📣
    Over-disclose everything. If there's something the seller or investor should know, tell them. Even if it's uncomfortable. Especially if it's uncomfortable.
