Module 5.10: Payout Process

Duration: 5 min • Operations
WHY THIS MATTERS: You need to know exactly how and when you get paid so you can manage your personal and business finances with confidence.

📖 LEARN

Understanding our payout structures ensures you know when to expect your commissions.

  • Normal: Paid on the 15th and the last day of the month. (The deal must fund at least 2 days prior to the payout date).
  • Expedited: Paid the same day for a 1% fee (minimum $50). Contact Michael DeMott at mdemott@trelly.com or (972) 849-7495 to request this.
  • RevShare: Paid on the last day of the month only. There is no expedited option for RevShare payouts.
