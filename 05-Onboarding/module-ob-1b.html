Membership Dues

Your monthly investment in tools, access, and support.

All agents are required to pay a monthly membership fee to maintain access to essential tools and services. Please review the details below and ensure your payment method is up to date.

Per agent, per month. Covers essential tools, CRM access, MLS support, and marketing resources.

$100

Additional fee if payment is not updated after a declined card notice. Keep your payment method current to avoid this charge.

Credit Card Authorization

Submit or update your credit card for automatic monthly billing. Your card will be charged on the 1st of each month.

Manage Your Payment Method
