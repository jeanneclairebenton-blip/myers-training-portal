Rules

SA-Specific Rules

Verify Before You Disclose

Never share a property address until the investor has signed the MVP agreement. No exceptions.

Keep It Off The Open Market

Never reveal the address or showing time in any public post — social media, ads, or listings.

TREC Logo Compliance

The Myers logo goes on all marketing materials where TREC requires it. Everything you publish must meet TREC requirements.

Report All Deals

All brokered or wholesaled transactions must be reported to Myers. Exception: deals you close to rent, flip, or hold yourself.

Compensation

Commission Splits

Commission Responsibility

If you ever receive a commission for real estate services, you are responsible for all brokerage splits — including when you're a principal to the transaction. This includes commissions from purchasing a listed property, helping another party buy or sell, and lease commissions.

Investor-first reward: Myers takes no broker fee on traditional brokerage referrals you pass to another agent.
