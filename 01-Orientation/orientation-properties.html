Columns

What You're Looking At

Each column on the board gives your investors the information they need to make fast, confident decisions.

Property Details

Beds, baths, square footage, lot size, and location. Every listing shows the basics at a glance.

Min Ask vs. Buy-It-Now

Min Ask = the starting bid for the auction. Buy-It-Now = skip the auction and purchase instantly at this price.

Showing Date & Time

When the 1-hour showing is scheduled. FCFS = First Come, First Serve (vacant property, no showing needed).

Estimated ARV & Rent

After-repair value and estimated monthly rent. These help your investors evaluate the deal before the showing.

Statuses

Status Guide

Each property has a status that tells you exactly where it is in the pipeline.

Active

Property is available for purchase. First Come, First Serve deals can be bought now.

Showing

A showing is scheduled. Attend, then bid via the Bid Form or pay Buy-It-Now price.

Pending

A contract is submitted and deposit collected. This deal is no longer available.

Check the board daily. New properties are added regularly. The fastest agents get the best deals to their investors first.
