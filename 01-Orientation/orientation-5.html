Property Flyers

Every property flyer is your investor's first impression. Here's what goes on one and why it matters.

What Goes on a Flyer

Every property flyer must include: property photos, price, square footage, bed/bath count, lot size, and the Myers logo. Flyers are created and distributed after a property is acquired.

Distribution

Marketing Channels

Get property flyers in front of the right investors through these three channels.

Investor Network

Send flyers directly to your verified investor list via email (GMass). Your network is your biggest asset.

Social Media

Post on Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn — but never include the property address in public posts.

Email / GMass

Use GMass to send bulk property flyers to your investor database. Fast, efficient, trackable.

Compliance

Compliance Reminders

Two non-negotiable rules for all marketing materials and public communications.

TREC Logo Required

The Myers logo must appear on all marketing materials where TREC requires it. No exceptions.

No Addresses in Public Posts

Never reveal property addresses or showing times in any public marketing — social media, ads, or listings.
