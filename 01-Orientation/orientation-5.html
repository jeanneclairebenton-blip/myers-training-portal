Property Flyers

What Goes on a Flyer

Every property flyer must include: property photos, price, square footage, bed/bath count, lot size, and the Myers logo. Flyers are created and distributed after a property is acquired.

Marketing Channels

Investor Network

Send flyers directly to your verified investor list via email (GMass).

Social Media

Post on Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn — but never include the property address in public posts.

Use GMass to send bulk property flyers to your investor database.

Compliance Reminders

TREC Logo Required

The Myers logo must appear on all marketing materials where TREC requires it.

No Addresses in Public Posts

Never reveal property addresses or showing times in any public marketing — social media, ads, or listings.
