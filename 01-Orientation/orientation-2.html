Purchase Methods

Two Ways to Buy

First Come, First Serve

  • Vacant properties or land
  • Can be bought at any time
  • The price you see is the price you pay

Showing (Silent Auction)

  • Occupied or seller-restricted
  • One scheduled 1-hour showing
  • Then choose: buy-now price or enter the auction
  • Required for properties in Dallas, Collin, Denton, Tarrant & Rockwall counties
Timeline

The Showing Timeline

1

Showing Window

= 1 Hour

One scheduled showing of the property. Investors view, inspect, and decide.

2

Bidding Closes

= 1 Hour

Bidding ends 1 hour after the end of the showing. Submit your best offer.

3

Secure Wire & Contracts

= 2 Hours

Winner has 2 hours after bidding ends to submit wire + contract, or the AA moves to the next buyer.

BUY IT NOW: Skip the auction entirely. Pay the posted buy-now price and the property is yours. To secure, agents must submit the investor's signed contract and wire transfer immediately.

Deposits

Securing with a Deposit

$5,000
Non-refundable deposit to secure a property
  • Refundable only if Myers cannot provide clear title
  • Due fast — within 1 hour of offer acceptance
  • Enforces respect for the process and the seller
  • Wire or cashier's check only. No exceptions.
Non-Negotiables

Investor-Agent Non-Negotiables

Bring Your Own EMD

Investors must provide their own earnest money deposit. No financing the deposit.

No Delaying the Deposit

Deposit is due within 1 hour. Delays forfeit the deal to the next buyer in line.

No Option Period

There is no option period. Once you commit, you are committed. Perform due diligence upfront.

Double Close

All transactions are structured as a double close. Myers sells to the investor as a principal.

Cash or Hard Money Only

No conventional or FHA financing. Investors must close with cash or hard money lending.

Close with Myers

All transactions close through Myers' designated title company. No outside closings.
