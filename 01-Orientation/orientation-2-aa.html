Rules

AA-Specific Rules

See It Before You Buy It

Don't acquire a property you haven't seen — unless the numbers are significantly de-risked.

Never Post Below Cost

Deals must not be posted for less than the price they were acquired for.

Verify Before You Disclose

Never share a property address until the investor is verified by signing the MVP agreement.

Confidentiality

The address and showing time are confidential. Never reveal them in any public marketing, social media, or listings.

Report All Deals

All brokered or wholesaled transactions must be reported. Exception: deals you close to rent, flip, or hold yourself.

TREC Compliance

The Myers logo goes on all marketing materials where TREC requires it. Stay compliant in everything you publish.

As an AA, you are the first line of quality control. Every deal you bring in reflects on the entire team.
