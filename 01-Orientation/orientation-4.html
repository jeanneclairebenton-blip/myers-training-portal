Verify Before You Disclose

Every time. No exceptions. Every investor must be verified before receiving any property address or showing details.

Protect the Deal. Protect Myers. Protect Yourself.

Protect the Deal — Confidential details stay private until the investor has skin in the game.

Documents

What Investors Sign

MVP Agreement

The core agreement that verifies the investor and grants access to property details. Must be signed before any address is shared.

Process Disclosures

Outlines how Myers operates — deposit requirements, showing rules, bidding process, and non-negotiables.

TREC Purchase Agreement

The standard Texas Real Estate Commission purchase contract used for all transactions.

First Addendum to Purchase

Additional terms specific to Myers transactions, including double close and funding requirements.
