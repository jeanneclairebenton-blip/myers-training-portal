Verifying Investors & NDA Review

Verify before you disclose — every time.
Why Verification Matters

Protect the Deal. Protect Myers. Protect Yourself.

Every investor must be verified before receiving any property address or showing details. This protects confidential deal information and ensures only serious, qualified buyers enter the process.

What Investors Sign

MVP Agreement

The core agreement that verifies the investor and grants access to property details. Must be signed before any address is shared.

Process Disclosures

Outlines how Myers operates — deposit requirements, showing rules, bidding process, and non-negotiables.

TREC Purchase Agreement

The standard Texas Real Estate Commission purchase contract used for all transactions.

First Addendum to Purchase

Additional terms specific to Myers transactions, including double close and funding requirements.

Before vs After Verification

BEFORE Verification

  • General property description only
  • No address
  • No showing time
  • No flyer with address
  • Marketing must be address-free

AFTER Verification

  • Full property address
  • Showing time & date
  • Detailed property flyer
  • Access to bid form
  • Full deal details

The address and showing time are confidential — protecting them protects the deal, the seller, and your commission.
