F.O.R.D. Framework

F.O.R.D. — Build Rapport Fast

F
Family
Ask about their family. Where are they from? Kids? This builds personal connection.
O
Occupation
What do they do for work? How long? This reveals their financial profile.
R
Recreation
What do they do for fun? Hobbies, travel, interests. Find common ground.
D
Dreams
What are their goals? Retirement plans? Investment aspirations? This is where you connect them to Myers.

Cold Call Basics

Your First Script

When calling a lead for the first time, follow this structure:

  1. Introduce yourself and Myers Home Buyers
  2. Ask if they're interested in investment properties in DFW
  3. Use F.O.R.D. to build rapport
  4. Qualify: Are they an active investor? What's their budget? Cash or financed?
  5. If qualified: "I'd love to get you set up to see our next available property."
  6. Collect: Name, email, phone, investment criteria
  7. Next step: Send MVP agreement for verification

Pro tip: The goal of the first call isn't to sell — it's to qualify and verify. Get them signed up, then let the deals do the selling.
