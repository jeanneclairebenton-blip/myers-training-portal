Rules

SA-Specific Rules

Verify Before You Disclose

Never share a property address until the investor has signed the MVP agreement. No exceptions.

Keep It Off The Open Market

Never reveal the address or showing time in any public post — social media, ads, or listings.

TREC Logo Compliance

The Myers logo goes on all marketing materials where TREC requires it. Everything you publish must meet TREC requirements.

Report All Deals

All brokered or wholesaled transactions must be reported to Myers. Exception: deals you close to rent, flip, or hold yourself.

Scripts

Common Investor Questions

What you'll hear most — and how Myers answers.

Q
Can I get in before showings?
Probably not. Showings are scheduled for a reason — to give every qualified buyer a fair shot. Early access undermines the process.
Q
Will you take $10K below buy-now right now?
No. The buy-now price is set. If you want to negotiate, enter the auction during the showing window.
Q
Will you take $3K as the deposit?
No. The deposit is $5,000 — non-refundable, wire or cashier's check only. No exceptions.
Q
Are closing dates negotiable?
No. Closing dates are firm and set by Myers.
Q
Can you assign the contract to me?
No. All transactions run as a double close through Myers' title company.
Q
Can you cover your side of closing costs?
No. Each party is responsible for their own closing costs.
Q
Will you pay my commission for a referral?
It depends. Contact your team lead to discuss referral commission structures on a case-by-case basis.
