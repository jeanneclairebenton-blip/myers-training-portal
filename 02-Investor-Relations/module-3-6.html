Repair Estimation

💰 Why This Matters

Accurate repair estimates prevent blown deals. Overestimate and you lose investors to competitors with better numbers. Underestimate and investors lose money — and trust in you.

Rehab Level Definitions

🟢 Light Rehab

  • Cleanup & declutter
  • Carpet replacement
  • Interior paint
  • Minor touch-ups

🟡 Average Rehab

  • Everything in Light, plus:
  • Countertops & fixtures
  • Updated finishes
  • Maybe 1 major system

🔴 Heavy Rehab

  • Everything in Average, plus:
  • Windows & roof
  • Foundation work
  • Full kitchen & flooring
