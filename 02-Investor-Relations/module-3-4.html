🧮 Module 3.4

Calculator Mastery: Cash ROI & Hard Money

⏱ 10 min 🏆 Milestone: Number Cruncher

💰 Why This Matters

Running numbers LIVE in a sit-down makes you indispensable to investors. When you can pull out a calculator and show them exactly what a deal looks like — cash vs leveraged — you become their go-to acquisition source.

📖 LEARN

Cash ROI Calculator

Cash ROI tells an investor: "If you put $X of your own money into this deal, you'll make $Y back — that's Z% return."

// Cash Deal Formula
Total Investment = Purchase Price + Repairs + Closing Costs
Profit = ARV − Total Investment − Selling Costs
Cash ROI = (Profit ÷ Total Investment) × 100

// Target: 20%+ ROI minimum on cash deals

Hard Money Calculator

Same deal, but now we add the cost of borrowed money. Hard money amplifies returns because you're using less of your own cash — but it also adds cost:

// Hard Money Formula — Additional Variables
Loan Amount = Typically 70-90% of purchase price
Points = 1-3 points upfront (1 point = 1% of loan)
Interest = Monthly rate × Loan Amount × Holding Period
Cash Out of Pocket = Down Payment + Repairs + Points + Interest + Closing

Hard Money ROI = (Profit ÷ Cash Out of Pocket) × 100
⚡ The Power of Leverage A deal that returns 20% ROI with cash can return 105%+ ROI with hard money — because your out-of-pocket is much smaller. Show investors this side-by-side and you'll blow their minds.

Example Deal: Side-by-Side Comparison

Line Item Cash Deal Hard Money
Purchase Price $120,000 $120,000
Repairs $30,000 $30,000
Closing Costs (Buy) $3,600 $3,600
Loan Amount (85%) — $102,000
Down Payment (15%) — $18,000
Points (2 pts) — $2,040
Interest (12%, 5 mo) — $5,100
ARV $210,000 $210,000
Selling Costs (8%) $16,800 $16,800
Total Out of Pocket $153,600 $58,740
Profit $39,600 $32,460
ROI 25.8% 55.3%
💡 Key Insight The hard money deal makes slightly less profit ($32K vs $39K) — but the ROI is more than double because the investor only put ~$59K of their own money at risk instead of ~$154K. That freed-up capital can fund another deal.
