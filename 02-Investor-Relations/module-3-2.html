🗣️ Module 3.2

Discovery Questions & F.O.R.D.

⏱ 8 min 🏆 Milestone: Question Master

💰 Why This Matters

The right questions make investors WANT to work with you — and only you. Great questions build trust faster than any pitch, and they reveal exactly what deals to bring to each investor.

📖 LEARN

The F.O.R.D. Framework

Before you get into strategy, build a human connection. The F.O.R.D. framework gives you four topics that instantly create rapport:

F

Family

Spouse, kids, where they grew up — shows you care about them as a person

O

Occupation

W-2 job, full-time investor, side hustle — reveals capacity & motivation

R

Recreation

Hobbies, travel, interests — creates common ground & memorable moments

D

Dreams

Goals, aspirations, vision — the emotional driver behind their investing

Rapport Questions

🤝 Build the Relationship First

  • "How did you get started in real estate investing?"
  • "What's been your biggest success so far?"
  • "What are your current challenges with investing?"
  • "What got you interested in this market?"
  • "Where do you see yourself in 5 years with investing?"

Strategy Questions

🎯 Understand Their Buy-Box

  • "What property types are you focused on?"
  • "What's your ideal price range?"
  • "Fix & flip, or buy & hold — or both?"
  • "Any preferred locations or zip codes?"
  • "What's the minimum ROI you target?"

Buying Process Questions

💳 Qualify Their Execution Ability

  • "Are you using cash or hard money for acquisitions?"
  • "How quickly can you typically close?"
  • "How often should I update you on new opportunities?"
  • "Do you have any partners who might want deals too?"
💡 Key Power Phrases
  • "How do you see us working together?"
  • "If we could double your volume, could you handle it?"
  • "Over the next 12 months, what would success look like for you?"
🔨 DO

Memorize Your Core Questions

Your goal: commit 5 rapport questions and 5 strategy questions to memory. You should be able to deliver them naturally — not like you're reading a list.

🏋️ Practice Method
  1. Read each question aloud 3 times
  2. Close this page and write them from memory
  3. Practice delivering them in conversational tone (not interrogation mode)
  4. Record yourself — listen for flow and naturalness
✅ PROVE

Match the Question Category

For each question below, identify whether it's a Rapport, Strategy, or Buying Process question.

1. "What's your ideal price range?"

2. "How did you get started in real estate investing?"

3. "Are you using cash or hard money?"

4. "Fix & flip, or buy & hold?"

5. "What's been your biggest success so far?"
